## 👥 HOW TO ADD REAL VOLUNTEERS:

### **Step 1: Get Volunteer Information**
For each volunteer, collect:
- **Name** (full name)
- **Role** (e.g., "Community Outreach Volunteer")
- **Photo** (professional headshot)
- **Quote** (short quote about their experience)

### **Step 2: Update careers.html**
Replace the placeholder sections:

```html
<!-- Replace this: -->
<h4>[Your Volunteer Name]</h4>
<p>[Volunteer Role]</p>
<p>"[Add your volunteer's quote]"</p>

<!-- With this: -->
<h4>Sarah Johnson</h4>
<p>Community Outreach Volunteer</p>
<p>"Volunteering at Green Africa Hub has been incredibly rewarding. I love seeing how sustainable cooking brings our community together."</p>
```

### **Step 3: Update Volunteer Images**

```html

<!-- With this: -->
```
