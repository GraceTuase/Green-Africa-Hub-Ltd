AI Carbon Tracker — Innovation

The UK's first AI-powered carbon tracking tool designed specifically for African and Caribbean cooking. Built by Grace using Python, Scikit-learn, and Power BI — the tool maps recipes to real emissions data using DEFRA 2025 factors and recommends culturally appropriate lower-carbon alternatives. Available to digital members via subscription. SaaS licensing planned for Year 3. Trademark application in preparation.

UK Mandatory Reporting & Net Zero Requirements

Sustainability is no longer optional - it's a business requirement

To secure contracts with councils, public bodies, and major supply chains, businesses must demonstrate real-time sustainability evidence. The UK's Net Zero goals and mandatory reporting requirements mean businesses need verifiable carbon data, not just theoretical commitments.

  • Streamlined Energy Carbon Reporting (SECR) - Mandatory for large UK companies
  • TCFD Disclosure - Climate-related financial risk reporting
  • Net Zero Strategy Requirements - Public sector procurement mandates
  • ESG Integration - Supply chain sustainability criteria
  • Green Claims Code - Avoiding greenwashing with verifiable evidence

💡 Key Insight: Businesses without real-time carbon tracking and ESG evidence are increasingly excluded from council contracts and public sector supply chains. Sustainability focus is now a competitive requirement, not just a nice-to-have.
