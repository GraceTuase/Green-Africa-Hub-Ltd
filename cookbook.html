Our Methodology

18-Month R&D Carbon Testing

Every recipe tested with DEFRA templates, induction hub technology, and real-world cooking scenarios.

DEFRA Carbon Templates

All carbon calculations use official UK DEFRA templates for food carbon footprinting, ensuring accuracy and compliance with national standards.

  • Official DEFRA methodology
  • UK-specific conversion factors
  • Transparent calculations
  • Peer-reviewed data sources

Induction Hub Technology

18-month R&D testing comparing induction hub vs traditional gas cooking. Energy efficiency, flavour preservation, and cultural authenticity maintained.

  • 60% average energy reduction
  • Authentic flavour preservation
  • Precise temperature control
  • Zero direct carbon emissions

Sustainable Supply Chain

All ingredients locally sourced or sea freighted. No air-freighted ingredients. UK Net Zero aligned sourcing practices and supplier partnerships.

  • Local sourcing priority
  • Sea freight only (no air)
  • Supplier sustainability training
  • Carbon footprint tracking
