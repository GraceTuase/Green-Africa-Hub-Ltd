Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and your choices

🍪 Cookie Policy

Green Africa Hub uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices.

🔍 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and provide relevant content.

📋 Types of Cookies We Use

🔒 Essential Cookies

Required for basic functionality:

  • User authentication and login status
  • Shopping cart contents
  • Security tokens
  • Payment processing

📊 Analytics Cookies

Help us understand usage:

  • Website traffic analysis
  • User behavior tracking
  • Performance monitoring
  • Content popularity

🎯 Functional Cookies

Enhance user experience:

  • Remember preferences
  • Language settings
  • Customized content
  • Saved forms data

⚙️ Your Cookie Choices

You have several options regarding cookies:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies for optimal experience
  • Reject Non-Essential: Allow only essential cookies
  • Browser Settings: Manage cookies through your browser
  • Cookie Banner: Change preferences anytime

🔐 Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (essential)
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (optional)
  • Social Media: Sharing buttons (optional)
  • YouTube: Video embedding (optional)

⏰ Cookie Duration

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when browser closes
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain for set period (days to months)
  • Authentication Cookies: 30 days for logged-in users
  • Preference Cookies: 365 days for user settings

📱 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Our cookie consent banner
  • Browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Browser extensions and add-ons
  • Device privacy settings

💡 Important Note

Rejecting non-essential cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience. Some features may not work as intended.

This Cookie Policy was last updated on April 4, 2026 and may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
