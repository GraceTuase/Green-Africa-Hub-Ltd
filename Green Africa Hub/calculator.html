The Science

Why Induction Wins

The physics and the grid are on our side.

⚡

85-90% Efficient

Induction hobs transfer 85-90% of energy directly to the pan via electromagnetic induction. Gas hobs waste 45-60% of energy as heat escaping around the pan.

🔌

UK Grid: 124g CO₂/kWh

The UK grid reached its cleanest ever in 2024 at 124g CO₂ per kWh — and it's getting cleaner every year. Gas stays at ~215g CO₂/kWh at the burner regardless.

🚢

Sea-Shipped = Low Carbon

Container shipping produces ~16g CO₂ per tonne-km vs ~500g for air freight. Most African and Caribbean staples — rice, yam, plantain, dried fish — travel by sea.

🔄

Smart Swaps, Not Sacrifice

We only substitute air-freighted ingredients like ugwu. Sea-shipped authentic ingredients remain exactly as tradition intended. British kale for ugwu cuts transport carbon by 99.9%.

🐐

Goat Meat

Lowest carbon red meat at 5kg CO₂e/kg — compared to 27kg for beef.

🐟

Mackerel

Lowest carbon protein at 0.7kg CO₂e/kg — abundant, affordable, and delicious.

🥬

British Kale

Replaces air-freighted ugwu with 99.9% less transport carbon — grown right here in the UK.
