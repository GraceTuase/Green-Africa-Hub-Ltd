The Carbon Badge System

Every Carbon Badge captures three categories of emissions: food production (farming, processing), ingredient transport (sea-shipped vs air-freighted), and cooking energy (induction vs gas). We use UK Government GHG conversion factors and real energy measurements from induction hobs.
