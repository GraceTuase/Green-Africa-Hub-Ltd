🎥 Video Usage & Copyright Guidelines

⚠️ Important Copyright Notice

Using others' videos without permission can lead to legal issues including:

  • Copyright infringement claims
  • Content removal requests
  • Legal action and fines
  • Platform bans or penalties

🛡️ Safe Video Usage Options

✅ RECOMMENDED: Create Your Own Content

Benefits: Full ownership, no copyright issues, authentic to your brand

  • Film your own cooking demonstrations
  • Show gas vs induction hub comparisons
  • Feature your community members
  • Demonstrate your unique recipes

🤝 PERMISSION-BASED USAGE

If you want to use others' content:

  1. Get Written Permission: Email the creator with specific usage request
  2. Credit Properly: Include creator name, handle, and link to original
  3. Check Platform Terms: Each platform has different reuse policies
  4. Consider Compensation: Offer payment or cross-promotion

📹 Technical Requirements

Video Specifications for Homepage

  • Format: MP4, WebM, or OGG
  • Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080) recommended
  • Length: 30 seconds - 3 minutes
  • File Size: Under 50MB for fast loading
  • Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (landscape)
  • Audio: Clear voiceover or music with proper licensing

⚖️ Legal Protection Steps

  1. Document Permissions: Keep email chains and written consent
  2. Create Credit System: Standardized attribution format
  3. Monitor Usage: Regular checks for copyright claims
  4. Have Backup Plan: Ready to remove content if requested
  5. Consider Insurance: Media liability coverage for business
