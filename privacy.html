Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

At Green Africa Hub, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

📋 Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, payment details
  • Account Information: Membership details, purchase history, preferences
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on site

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To provide memberships, workshops, and events
  • Communication: To send updates, newsletters, and service information
  • Payment Processing: To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Website Improvement: To analyze usage and improve our services
  • Legal Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory requirements

🔐 Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes SSL encryption, secure payment processing, and regular security audits.

🍪 Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can accept or reject cookies through our cookie consent banner. Essential cookies are required for basic functionality.
