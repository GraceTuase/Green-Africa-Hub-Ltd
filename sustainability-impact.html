Our Sustainability Framework

Four Pillars of Environmental Impact

We combine cultural preservation with environmental responsibility to create sustainable solutions that work for African and Caribbean communities.

Electric Hub Cooking Revolution

Transforming traditional cooking methods with modern electric hub technology that preserves flavour while eliminating environmental impact.

Key Benefits:

  • 60% energy cost reduction vs gas cooking
  • Zero direct carbon emissions
  • Precise temperature control for authentic flavours
  • Safer cooking environment for families
  • Compatible with traditional African/Caribbean techniques

Gas-to-Electric Transition

Comprehensive programme to help households and commercial kitchens eliminate gas cooking while maintaining cultural authenticity and improving economic outcomes.

Transition Support:

  • Free electric hub cooker demonstrations
  • Subsidy guidance for equipment upgrades
  • Traditional technique adaptation training
  • Energy cost savings calculations
  • Carbon footprint reduction tracking

Zero Food Waste Initiative

Smart-Prep methodology and community policies that ensure no food goes to waste while reducing household costs and environmental impact.

Waste Reduction Strategies:

  • Take-home containers for all event attendees
  • Bulk cooking and sharing economies
  • Smart storage and preservation techniques
  • Leftover recipe transformation workshops
  • Community food sharing networks

Plastic-Free Community Cooking

Eliminating single-use plastics through community policies, reusable container systems, and sustainable packaging alternatives.

Plastic Reduction Methods:

  • Bring-your-own-container policies
  • Reusable container lending library
  • Bulk ingredient purchasing programmes
  • Biodegradable packaging alternatives
  • Community container sharing networks
