Event Policies

Important Information

Please review our event policies before booking.

Booking & Cancellation

  • Early booking recommended (100 capacity limit)
  • 7-day cancellation policy for full refund
  • Transfers allowed up to 48 hours before event
  • No-shows charged full price
  • Group bookings (5+) receive 25% discount

Dietary Requirements

  • Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available
  • Allergen information provided for each dish
  • Notify dietary requirements 48 hours in advance
  • Halal options available upon request
  • Nut-free environment (no nuts used in preparation)

Sustainability Requirements

  • Bring your own containers for takeaway (plastic reduction)
  • Reusable bags encouraged for bulk sharing
  • Food waste elimination policy
  • Carbon-neutral event certification
  • Local and sustainable sourcing only

Accessibility & Inclusion

  • Wheelchair accessible venue
  • Disabled parking available
  • Assistance animals welcome
  • Large print menu available
  • Cultural sensitivity training for all staff
